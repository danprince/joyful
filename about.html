joyful.club is a tiny website for sharing joy with others.

Clubs

Each club has its own URL. Here are some examples:

  • joyful.club/books
  • joyful.club/🐱
  • joyful.club/adventures

🤫 If you want a super-secret club with your friends, pick something no one will guess.

  • joyful.club/blue-flamingos-at-dawn

Here are the things you should know before posting:

  • 📝 Posts can't be longer than 100 characters.
  • ♻️ A new post will replace your previous post.
  • 👻 Posts are entirely anonymous.
  • 😊 Posts aren't moderated, so be nice!

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where can I ask questions? In joyful.club/faq.
  • Who can create a club? Anyone. Just visit the URL of the club and start posting.
  • Is there a list of all clubs? Nope. It's not a social network.
  • Can I see all of my own posts? Nope. 👆
  • Can I see all of the posts in a club? Nope, just one at a time! 👆
  • Who can see my posts? Anyone with the URL of the club you posted them in.
  • What order are the posts shown in? Totally random.
  • How does it work? The code is here on GitHub.
